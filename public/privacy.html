Privacy notice — pre-launch draft

This document is not ready for public launch. The registered company, providers, retention periods, lawful bases and contact details must be completed and reviewed by UK counsel.

Our privacy promise

TraceGuard is designed to help adult creators locate suspected unauthorised uses of content they control. We minimise collection, encrypt reference media and require creator approval before enforcement actions.

Information processed

Account details, public creator aliases and profile URLs, encrypted reference media, potential-match evidence, case communications, subscription status, security and audit records may be processed. Facial templates will remain disabled until a separate lawful-basis assessment, Article 9 condition, DPIA and explicit activation flow are approved.

Rights

UK users may have rights of access, correction, deletion, restriction, objection and portability, and may complain to the Information Commissioner's Office. Operational contact details will be inserted before launch.

Retention and sharing

Final retention periods and the provider list will be published before launch. Data will not be sold. Providers will only receive the minimum information necessary under written contracts and appropriate transfer safeguards.
