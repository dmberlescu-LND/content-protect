The service is intended only for verified adults aged 18 or over who own the relevant rights or are authorised to act for the rights holder.

Users must not search for, monitor or submit claims concerning another person without authority. False copyright claims, harassment, unlawful surveillance and upload of illegal content are prohibited.

A similarity result is not proof of infringement. Users must review evidence and confirm ownership before any notice. Delisting a result from a search engine does not remove it from the source website. Outcomes and response times cannot be guaranteed.

Prices, renewal, cancellation, refunds and VAT treatment will be presented before checkout. Current billing is sandbox-only and no payment is taken.
