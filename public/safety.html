Creator safety

If you are in immediate danger

Contact emergency services. Content Protect is not an emergency or law-enforcement service.

Intimate-image abuse

Do not contact suspected uploaders directly if that may increase risk. Preserve URLs and timestamps, secure your accounts and seek specialist support. A dedicated UK resource directory and urgent reporting workflow will be added before launch.

Account safety

Use a unique password, enable two-factor authentication when available and never give Content Protect the password to a creator platform. We only request public profile links.
