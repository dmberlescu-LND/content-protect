Creator safety

Immediate danger: call 999 in the UK. For non-emergency police assistance call 101 or use your local police's online reporting service. Content Protect is not an emergency or law-enforcement service.

Intimate-image abuse

Do not contact a suspected uploader if doing so may increase risk. Preserve URLs, usernames, timestamps and messages without repeatedly downloading or sharing the content. Secure your email, cloud storage and creator accounts.

  • Adults in the UK: contact the Revenge Porn Helpline.
  • Under 18, or an image taken when under 18: use Report Remove. Content Protect is an adult-only service.
  • Sexual images of anyone under 18 should be reported to the Internet Watch Foundation; do not upload them here.
  • For broader online abuse, use the platform report tool and Report Harmful Content.

Blackmail, stalking or domestic abuse

Do not pay or negotiate without specialist advice. Preserve communications, review location sharing and linked devices, and contact police or a specialist support service. If an abuser may monitor your device, seek help from a safer device.

Account safety

Use a unique password, protect your email account, review active sessions and never provide Content Protect with a creator-platform password. We only request public profile links.

Report a safety concern

Email white.eagles.dm@gmail.com. Do not attach intimate files to ordinary email; send URLs and a brief description. For a takedown dispute, use the dispute channel.
